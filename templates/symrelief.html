Symptom Relief

Headache

  • Stay hydrated with plenty of water.
  • Apply a cold or warm compress to your forehead.
  • Rest in a dark, quiet room.
  • Consider over-the-counter pain relievers.

Back Pain

  • Apply heat or cold packs to the affected area.
  • Maintain good posture and use ergonomic chairs.
  • Perform gentle stretching exercises.
  • Consult a physical therapist for targeted exercises.

Joint Pain

  • Engage in low-impact exercises like swimming.
  • Use supportive braces or wraps.
  • Apply topical anti-inflammatory creams.
  • Consider dietary supplements like glucosamine.
